Cyber Security Blog

Latest insights, news & awareness from the cyber world

The Rise of AI Cyber Attacks

Artificial intelligence is now being used by hackers to automate attacks.

AI-driven attacks include automated phishing, deepfakes, and adaptive malware. AI-based defense systems counter these threats using behavior analysis and machine learning.

Top 5 Password Mistakes

Most breaches occur due to weak passwords.

Avoid reusing passwords, using names or dates, storing passwords in plain text, ignoring two-factor authentication, and sharing credentials.

Social Engineering: Human Weakness

Scammers manipulate people instead of systems.

Techniques include phishing, baiting, pretexting, and impersonation. Awareness is the strongest defense.

Is Public WiFi Safe?

Public WiFi is often insecure and unencrypted.

Always use VPN, avoid online banking, and disable file sharing when connected to public WiFi.

Why Businesses Need Cyber Security

Small businesses are targeted daily by cybercriminals.

Cybersecurity protects customer data, prevents financial loss, ensures compliance, and builds trust.

The Rise of Ransomware Attacks

Ransomware locks your data and demands payment.

Regular backups, patching systems, and email filtering reduce ransomware risks.

Protecting Personal Data in the Digital Age

Apps and websites collect more data than ever.

Use strong privacy settings, avoid oversharing, and encrypt sensitive files.

Cloud Security Myths Debunked

The cloud is not automatically secure.

Cloud security depends on correct configuration, access control, encryption, and monitoring.
